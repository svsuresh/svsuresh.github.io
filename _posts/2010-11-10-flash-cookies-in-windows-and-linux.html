--- layout: post title: Delete persistent flash cookies in windows and linux date: '2010-11-10T23:34:00.001+05:30' author: ssv tags: - clearing flash cookies modified_time: '2014-07-17T15:38:00.519+05:30' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3175511529662794199.post-2607008036522978605 blogger_orig_url: https://digibio.blogspot.com/2010/11/flash-cookies-in-windows-and-linux.html --- Well, most of the cache cleaning software gets rid of cookies except for flash cookies. Many security experts (excluding me :D) pointed out that these may be security risk. These persistent flash cookies stored some where else other than your temporary internet files (IE) and/or profile (Firefox).

In windows 7, flash cookies are in C:\users\Your_login_ID\AppData\Roaming\Macromedia\Flash Player\macromedia.com\support\flashplayer\sys\ and you can empty the folder.


In Ubuntu, they lie in /home/Your_login_ID/.macromedia/Flash_Player/macromedia.com/support/flashplayer/sys.
